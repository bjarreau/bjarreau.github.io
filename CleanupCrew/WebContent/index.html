The Clean-Up Crew is a zombie survival game like no other. Don't just survive the zombie apocalypse, play a key role in ending it for good in this augmented reality game. Use the image markers provided on our images page to out smart incoming zombies and increase your odds of success.
Download Instructions: From Your Mobile Device: Ensure your device is open to third party sources. Click on the "Download" Button. Use a File Explorer to Open the downloaded apk file. Then install on device.
How to Play:

Start

Once you start the game, you will place the player image target on the surface within the view of the camera. When the camera detects the image, your player will appear and the game will begin.

Game Play

Move your player throughout the scene by pressing the up, down, left, or right buttons on the left of the screen. Tap anywhere on the screen to shoot in that direction.

Adding Objects

Add the respective image target to insert an obstacle into the game.

Leveling Up

Between waves, you will be provided with a count down to incoming, a health boost, and a level update.
